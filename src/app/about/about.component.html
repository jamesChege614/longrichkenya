our history

Longrich is an international manufacturing company founded in 1986 with its headquarters in China.It has offices in over 190 countries globally. It has nine world class research and development centres based in China, France, America, Africa and Japan. Longrich landed in Africa seven years ago in Lagos Nigeria. Its first office was opened in Kenya in 2017 in Nairobi and since it has spread all over the country with Nakuru, Kisumu, Eldoret, Kakamega, Mombasa e.t.c being branch ambassodors.
